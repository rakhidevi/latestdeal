LatestDeal AI Engine

📖 How to use the LatestDeal Control Center (Click to expand)
  • Server Worker (Core): Must be running to process the queue. It takes pending deals, extracts data via Crawl4AI, evaluates them, generates AI captions, and pushes to Laravel.
  • Desktop Worker (Browser Fallback): Turn this on if the Server Worker hits CAPTCHAs. It launches a physical Chrome browser on your screen to manually bypass bot protections.
  • Telegram Scraper: Turn this on to actively listen to your Telegram channels and automatically ingest any deal links posted there.
  • AI Hunter (Auto-Scraper): A scheduled bot that searches Amazon for keywords/categories and automatically adds them to the queue.
    • Premium Tracking (SiteStripe): Extracts short links via your logged-in Amazon account before deep scraping.
    • Standard Ingestion: Directly scrapes URLs.
