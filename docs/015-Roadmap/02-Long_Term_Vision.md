# Long-Term Vision (3-5 Years)

**ID:** REQ-RDM-002
**Status:** Completed
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-29

## 1. The Global Network
Expand beyond local/regional eCommerce (e.g., just US or just India) to support dynamic geographic deal routing based on the Publisher's audience location.

## 2. White-Label Mobile Apps
Allow "Pro" tier publishers to generate their own custom-branded iOS/Android deal apps powered entirely by the LatestDeal backend API.

## 3. B2B Merchant Portal
Allow brands and merchants to bypass the scraping engine entirely and submit deals directly into the LatestDeal database for priority distribution.
